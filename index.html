Brush Clearing

Struggling with an overgrowth of tall grasses, invasive weeds, blackberry bushes, or encroaching small trees on your property? The task of reclaiming your land doesn't have to be a daunting one. At Pacific Land Preservation, we understand the challenges homeowners face with overgrown lots and offer a sustainable and environmentally friendly solution that steers clear of traditional methods like burning, hauling away debris, or extensive digging. Our innovative approach involves a thorough mulching process that not only eliminates unwanted vegetation but also turns it into valuable nutrients that are returned to the soil. This method promotes the health and growth of the remaining trees and plants, enhancing your property's ecosystem.

Serving the communities of Woodinville, Redmond, and Fall City, our mission is to assist property owners in transforming neglected or overrun areas into usable, vibrant spaces. Whether you're facing a small, troublesome spot or dealing with acres of untamed land, our team is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and the expertise necessary to tackle any size project. We pride ourselves on delivering a service that not only meets your land clearing needs but also supports the natural balance of your outdoor environment.
